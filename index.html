Welcome to Course Organizer

This website is designed to help students organize their courses easily and efficiently. You can view your courses, add new ones, and keep track of important information.

Features

  • View all your courses in one place
  • Add new courses
  • Organize course details like instructor and credit hours

Why use this website?

Managing courses manually can be confusing. This website provides a simple solution to keep everything organized in one place with a clean and responsive design.
